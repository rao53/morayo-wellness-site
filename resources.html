Healing from Trauma: What You Should Know

What is Trauma? Emotional pain after frightening, violent, or overwhelming events. Experts note it can come from abuse, accidents, loss, or witnessing violence.

Normal Reactions: Nightmares, sadness, anger, fear, guilt. These are common responses‚Äînot signs of weakness.

Why it matters in Nigeria: High rates of violence mean many people experience these symptoms.

Cultural Coping Tips: Talk to a trusted elder or spiritual advisor; pray or use faith-based groups; confide in a trusted friend; try journaling or expressive writing.

When to Seek Help: If feelings don‚Äôt improve after several weeks, therapy helps. Many people feel better within weeks of starting.

Grief & Postpartum Mental Health

Coping with Grief

  • Grief is a natural response to loss (after abuse, miscarriage, death, divorce, etc.).
  • Healing is supported by talking, resting, and remembering.
  • Talk about the person or event. Light a candle or plant something to remember them.
  • Rest, eat, and allow yourself to cry.

After Baby: Taking Care of Your Mental Health

  • About 1 in 5 new mothers feel sad, anxious, or disconnected (postpartum depression). It‚Äôs not your fault.
  • Get support early. Talking helps. Therapy helps. You‚Äôre not alone.
  • Look for signs: feeling hopeless, not bonding with baby, low energy.
  • Morayo can connect you to care.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can access services?

Any woman or girl in Nigeria affected by trauma. No income, insurance, or paperwork barrier.

How do I choose between online and in-person?

Choose what's safest and most convenient. Both offer quality, confidential care.

Can I get help without a smartphone?

Yes. We offer both video call therapy and in-person sessions.

What if I can't afford transportation?

We offer transport stipends, just mention this in your intake form.

Are sessions confidential?

Absolutely. All therapists follow professional ethical codes.

How long are sessions?

Standard sessions are 60 minutes, with 90 minutes available if needed.

How do we know it works?

Research shows reminders and digital tools improve therapy access and outcomes. We collect session data and feedback to refine our services in real time.

Still have questions?

We're here to help. Reach out anytime.
