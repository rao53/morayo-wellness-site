Who We Are

Morayo Wellness Initiative is a nonprofit founded in 2025, born out of a personal mission to ensure every woman and girl in Nigeria has access to high-quality, trauma-informed mental health care.

Our name, Morayo, means "I see joy again," reflecting our commitment to help survivors of abuse, early marriage, and pregnancy trauma rediscover strength and hope.
